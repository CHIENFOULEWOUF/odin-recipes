Pizza dough!

Can be used for all kinds of recipe

Ingredients
  • Flour : 350gr and another 100gr
  • 2 big spoon of olive oil
  • Some yeast
  • A cup of lukewarm water
  • Salt

Instructions
  1. Pour the first 350gr of flour, the olive oil, yeast and salt.
  2. Pour the water little by little while mixing with your hands, while slowly incorporating the rest of the flour.
  3. When the crust is well mixed, knead for about 5-10 min until it has some elasticity
  4. Let the crust grow for 1 hour
